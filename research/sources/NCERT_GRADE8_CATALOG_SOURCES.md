# Grade 8 curriculum seed sources

The database stores chapter/unit titles, source links, and short authored concept labels. It does **not** store textbook passages, exercises, or answer keys.

| Subject | Source used | Seeded scope |
|---|---|---|
| Mathematics | [NCERT Mathematics Textbook for Class VIII, 2024–25 reprint](https://ncert.nic.in/textbook/pdf/hemh1ps.pdf) | 13 listed chapters; 39 concept labels |
| Science | [NCERT Science Textbook for Class VIII, 2024–25 reprint](https://ncert.nic.in/textbook/pdf/hesc1ps.pdf) | 13 listed chapters; 39 concept labels |
| English | [NCERT Honeydew Textbook for Class VIII, 2024–25 reprint](https://ncert.nic.in/textbook/pdf/hehd1ps.pdf) | 8 listed units; 24 language/reading concept labels |

The 2024–25 NCERT tables of contents were used for unit metadata. English concept labels are newly authored skill labels; they are not reproductions of textbook content. Curriculum scope can be replaced later for another board without changing the frontend data contract.
